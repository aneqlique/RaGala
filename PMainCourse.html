Sisig Crocodile

 
Thriving in the island's ecosystem, crocodile meat is a popular choice for its taste and health benefits. Often described as similar to chicken with a firmer texture, it's both delicious and rich in protein while being low in fat. Craving a twist on a Filipino classic? Try crocodile sisig! This dish uses crocodile instead of pork, offering a lighter and more exotic take on the sizzling favorite.

Danggit Lamayo

 
This delicious treat features strips of local fish, semi-dried and marinated in a vibrant blend. Imagine the delicate sweetness of the fish mingling with savory notes and a hint of tang. Unlike other dried fish, Danggit Lamayo boasts a unique, slightly moist texture thanks to the marinating process. Typically served with a simple vinegar sauce spiked with chilies and peppercorns, it's a perfect appetizer or a delightful snack to enjoy with a cold beer.
