D E S S E R T

Try out some sweet desserts from Palawan!

CREDITS TO : Taste of the Place

Baker Hill's Hopia

 
Imagine flaky, golden rolls filled with a variety of delicious flavors. From the classic sweetness of monggo (mung bean) to the unique creaminess of ube (purple yam), Baker's Hill offers you Hopia with extraordinary ube filling inside giving you an extensive sweetness to tantalize your taste buds.

Super Creamy Halo-halo

 
This vibrant shaved ice dessert combines sweetened beans, fruits, and a scoop of ice cream. Each spoonful is a delightful surprise, bursting with flavor and textures. Enjoy this refreshing, extremely creamy, and iconic treat to cool down after exploring, only at Puerto Princesa!

CREDITS TO : Mae Illagan

CREDITS TO : Cuyo Island Delicacies

Kombo

 
This local delicacy features thinly sliced, unripe bananas that are patiently sun-dried or cooked over low heat until they achieve a chewy texture and a beautiful brown color. Unlike sugary banana chips, Kombo boasts a subtle sweetness and a satisfying chew. This is from the island of Cuyo only in Palawan Province. Try this snack now!
